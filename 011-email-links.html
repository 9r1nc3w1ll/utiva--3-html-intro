Email Links in HTML

Basic Email Link

Send Email

Code: <a href="mailto:contact@example.com">Send Email</a>

Email with Subject Line

Email with Subject

Code: <a href="mailto:contact@example.com?subject=Website%20Inquiry">Email with Subject</a>

Note: The space in "Website Inquiry" is URL-encoded as %20

Email with Subject and Body

Email with Subject and Body

Code: <a href="mailto:contact@example.com?subject=Website%20Inquiry&body=Hello%2C%20I%20have%20a%20question.">Email with Subject and Body</a>

URL encoding in the body:

  • Space = %20
  • Comma = %2C
  • Line break = %0A
  • Question mark = %3F
  • And so on...

Email with Multiple Recipients

Email to Multiple Recipients

Code: <a href="mailto:primary@example.com,cc@example.com">Email to Multiple Recipients</a>

Email with CC and BCC

Email with CC and BCC

Code: <a href="mailto:primary@example.com?cc=cc@example.com&bcc=bcc@example.com">Email with CC and BCC</a>
